Summary: City Latitude vs. Cloudiness


The purpose of this analysis was to evaluate city cloudiness dependency on latitude. All 500 randomly chosen cities' latitudes were plotted w.r.t recorded cloudiness. From the chart, one can see there is no clear dependency on city latitude vs. cloudiness. At every latitude point, cloudiness ranges from 0 - ~100% with no outlier data points. The only point to note is that there is a greater variation in city cloudiness for cities closer to the north pole than there is for cities nearer to the south pole (these cities tend to be extremely clear or extremely cloudy based on recorded data).
