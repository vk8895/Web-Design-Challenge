Summary: City Latitude vs. Temperature


The purpose of this analysis was to evaluate city temperature dependency on latitude. All 500 randomly chosen cities' latitudes were plotted w.r.t highest recorded temperatures over the past year. From the analysis, one can a reverse U pattern of temperatures across latitudes. Cities with lower temperatures are far away from 0 Latitude (equator), while cities with higher temperatures tend to be clustered around the equator. This makes sense with what we know about general climates; it is hotter near the equator and colder by the poles.
